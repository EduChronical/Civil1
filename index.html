The System is Rigged. Here is the Proof.

For decades, the State Bank of India (SBI) has been marketed as the bedrock of trust for the Indian middle class. We were told it was the "Banker to Every Indian." We were lied to.

In Why SBI Betrayed India, researcher and rationalist Aguru Manoj Kumar strips away the corporate propaganda to reveal a chilling reality: a multi-billion-rupee machinery designed to siphon wealth from the many to the few.

About the Author: Aguru Manoj Kumar is an author, researcher, and civil engineer. A former Scale-1 Officer at CGGB, he applies a rigorous, evidence-based methodology to investigate systemic corruption.
