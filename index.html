4. PRODUCT LIABILITY & PROPERTY DAMAGE

Forensic electrical testing of the Lenovo 65W adapter confirmed critical manufacturing defects (unstable voltage output, internal circuit failure) that caused an uncontrolled electrical surge. This surge directly resulted in:

  • Total destruction of 4 high-end laptops (combined value: ₹10,71,990/-).
  • Fire in one device (MSI Titan 18 HX AI) posing a life risk to the complainant and his family.
  • Complete professional work paralysis for 20+ days and permanent loss of critical data.

Forensic Conclusion: The defective Lenovo adapter is the sole and proximate cause of all physical injuries and property damage.
